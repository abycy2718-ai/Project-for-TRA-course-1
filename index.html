Our Story

Hong Kong is a gateway for global business, offering connectivity, talent, and opportunity.

B+H Architects, a Canadian design practice, established its Hong Kong presence in 2012 with just one employee. Today, its Hong Kong office has grown into a vibrant studio of more than 20 architects, leading landmark projects in the Greater Bay Area, including the Shenzhen Natural History Museum and the Shenzhen Children’s Hospital.

Now part of Surbana Jurong Group, B+H advances its global design vision, empowered by Hong Kong’s open business environment — proving the city is the ideal base for international growth in Asia.

我们的故事

香港是全球商务的门户，集连通力、人才资源和发展机遇于一身。

来自加拿大的 B+H 建筑设计事务所 于 2012 年在港设点。当时虽只有一名员工，而如今这里已成长为一个拥有二十多位建筑师的活力团队！并且其亦在大湾区主导多个地标项目，包括深圳自然博物馆和深圳儿童医院。

加入 盛裕集团（Surbana Jurong Group） 后，B+H 继续坚持其国际化设计视野，并在香港开放的营商环境助力下不断前行，印证了香港是国际企业立足和拓展亚洲的理想平台。
